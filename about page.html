OUR STORY

Where smoke meets honey and tradition meets the plate.

Born from the Ember

Founded in 2018, Honeywood Smokehouse started with a simple mission: to bring authentic, low-and-slow wood-fired BBQ to the heart of the community. What began as a backyard passion project involving a custom-built offset smoker and a secret honey-based rub has grown into a local landmark for meat lovers.

We believe that great BBQ cannot be rushed. Our Pitmasters arrive at 3:00 AM every morning to light the fires, using only locally sourced seasoned oak and hickory. This dedication to the craft ensures that every slice of brisket and every rib we serve carries the deep, smoky soul of Honeywood.

The Pitmaster's Way

🔥

The Wood

We use a blend of seasoned White Oak for consistent heat and Hickory for that classic, robust smoke profile.

🍯

The Glaze

Our signature honey-glaze is harvested from local apiaries, providing a floral sweetness that cuts through the rich fat.

⏳

The Time

No electric pellets or gas assists. Just fire, meat, and up to 14 hours of patience for every single batch.

Visit the Smokehouse

Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a full family feast, our doors are open. Come smell the smoke and join the Honeywood family.
